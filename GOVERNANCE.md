# Governance

How decisions get made across **chessvision-org**. This is intentionally light —
the goal is to keep the projects healthy and welcoming, not to add process for
its own sake.

## Roles

- **Maintainers** review and merge changes, triage issues, cut releases, and set
  technical direction. They are responsible for the long-term health of the
  projects.
- **Contributors** are anyone who opens an issue or pull request. You don't need
  permission to contribute — see [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md).

## How decisions are made

- Most changes happen through pull requests reviewed by a maintainer.
- Larger or contentious changes start as an issue or discussion so the reasoning
  is public and others can weigh in.
- Maintainers aim for consensus. When that isn't reached, the maintainers make
  the final call, guided by the project's mission: open-source, forever-free,
  and privacy-first.

## Becoming a maintainer

There's no formal application. Contributors who show consistent, high-quality
work and good judgment over time may be invited to become maintainers.

## Changes to governance

This document can be updated by the maintainers via pull request. Substantial
changes should be opened as a discussion first.
