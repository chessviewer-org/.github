# Support

Need help with a chessvision-org project? Here's where to go.

## Questions and discussion

- **GitHub Discussions** — for questions, ideas, and general help. Check the
  relevant repository's **Discussions** tab.
- **Search first** — your question may already be answered in existing issues or
  discussions.

## Found a bug?

Open an **issue** in the affected repository and use the bug report template.
Clear steps to reproduce help us fix it faster.

## Have a feature idea?

Open an **issue** with the feature request template. Describe the problem you're
trying to solve, not just the solution — it leads to better outcomes.

## Security issue?

Don't open a public issue. Follow our [Security Policy](./SECURITY.md) and report
it privately.

## Still stuck?

Email **contact@chessvision.org**. We're a small project, so please be patient —
but we read everything.

---

Please keep all interactions civil and on-topic, per our
[Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
